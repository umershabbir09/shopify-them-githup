Shortcodes for Product Description

You have 6 shortcodes.
You could use Editor mode or Show HTML mode

Product short description shortcode (Optional)

Product description shortcode in Product description tab (Optional)

Product New Badge shortcode. Put day limit, how many days show the new badge in your product.

Affiliate link product short code in "Add to cart icon"

Product video shortcode in Product video tab

Product unique tab shortcode in Product tab

Product unique tab shortcode in Product tab

Product unique tab shortcode in Product tab

Product countdown shortcode.


  1. [short_description]Here add your product short description[/short_description] - work on Product page below the product condition area, List product list view page, Search page and quick view
  2. [product_description]Here add your product full description[/product_description] - work on Product page in product description tab
  3. [new_products]Here add your product New Badge day limit[/new_products] - work on Product Grid and List view
  4. [affiliate_link]Here add your affiliate link[/affiliate_link] - work on Product page in "Add to cart" action
  5. [product_video]YouTube Product Video ID[/product_video] - work on Product page in video tab
  6. [unique_description_1] Your content or text here [/unique_description_1] - work on Product page in Unique-tab-1
  7. [unique_description_2] Your content or text here [/unique_description_2] - work on Product page in Unique-tab-2
  8. [unique_description_2] Your content or text here [/unique_description_3] - work on Product page in Unique-tab-3
  9. [countdown]YYYY/MM/DD[/countdown] - Here's how long the countdown times you want to see
